My Vision

Why AI, and why responsibly

For over ten years I built and advised on enterprise technology for large international companies. A few years ago I found the work I want to do for the rest of my career: Artificial Intelligence.

I believe AI is the most important technology of our generation — and that it has to be built and deployed responsibly. That conviction drives everything I do. At AXA I evaluate where AI can genuinely add value across insurance and SAP processes. In my own time I design and ship LLM-based products. And together with Prof. Dr. Philippe Tamla I work on AI research.

As a native Lingala speaker, one part of that research is personal: helping modern language models understand under-resourced African languages, so that the next generation of AI speaks to more of the world.

My goal is simple — to keep going deeper: from integrating AI, to shaping how it works.

Research & Collaboration

Bringing African languages into AI

I collaborate with Prof. Dr. Philippe Tamla — Professor of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on Information Extraction, NLP and LLM-based applications — on joint AI projects.

Our current project extends real-time translation technology to under-resourced African languages such as Igbo and Lingala. The goal is to integrate African languages more deeply into AI- and LLM-based translation — an area that today’s models still serve poorly.

Status: in active development
